About Piped Beats

Piped Beats is, speaking clearly, a simple client of SoundCloud that allows you to listen for free to streamed music, selected using your preferences (either by keywords or by genre).

The idea behind this project isn't to create an advanced streaming radio. It's just to experiment with new technologies to do a browser-based client for a well-known API.

Why create a client to stream music? Well, the real question is: why not?.
